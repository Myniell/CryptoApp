Application Functionalities

  • Have a secure login/signup system.
  • Be able to track crypto prices in real time.
  • Own a portfolio of different types of crypto and track the changing values.
  • Have the possibility to check price history under the form of a graph for each coin.
  • Trade currency at any time
  • Compete with others based on profit per time estimate
  • Offer the user more customizable options (ex: light/dark mode dashboard, different layout, enable notifications etc...)
